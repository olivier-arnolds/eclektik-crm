**Version:** 1.0 · **Date:** 2026-06-09 · **Author:** Marco + Claude (Cowork session)
**Baseline:** PS DB at v0.10.0 (Phase 1 rebuild complete — 14 of 22 clients with ACM + JDR grounded in raw IR decks; 7 pre-IR/pre-contract correctly empty; Chugai zCLOSED). ## Thesis

People Science today is a structured insight repository — best-in-class qualitative deliverables with provenance discipline. The next level is an **analytics engine with a commercial moat**: item-level data under every narrative, a proprietary cross-client pattern library, and evidence that our advice moves scores. Three tables carry the moat: `items`, `patterns`, and (new) NBA outcomes.

ACM analyses are fully populated on strategy_mapping / metrics_dashboard / counter_signals; JDR sits at 1-2 of 29. Decide: backfill JDR via the skill, or declare JDR narrative-only and null those fields by design. Either is fine; half-migrated rots. Log the decision.

### W5 — NBA outcome loop (next quarter)

Small schema addition: `nba_outcomes (analysis_id, nba_index, status: adopted/partial/not-adopted, target_item, next_cycle_delta, note)`. At every new cycle, the analyse skill scores last cycle's Top-3 NBAs against the new item data (possible once W2 lands). Output after 2-3 cycles: "clients who executed our NBAs moved the targeted item by X vs Y for those who didn't" — the strongest commercial claim a consultancy can make. Westfalen's improvement-yet-closed paradox shows why outcomes ≠ scores must be tracked explicitly.

### W6 — Pipeline scale-out (next quarter)

Wire `analysis_queue` end-to-end: deck lands on SharePoint → queue row → skill-driven draft (ACM + JDR + items + pattern tags) → Marco reviews → `status='shared'`. Unit economics move from hours-per-IR to review-minutes-per-IR; that is what lets the portfolio grow past ~25 clients without quality loss.

## The commercial asset (12-month horizon)

Glint gives clients their own data. Nobody gives them: "across N comparable organisations, here is what cycle 2-3-4 typically looks like, which patterns you exhibit, what peers did about them, and what moved." Items (W2) + patterns (W3) + outcomes (W5) make that productisable — as a premium tier of the IR engagement or a standalone benchmark read for pre-IR prospects.

## Conventions (binding for all workstreams)

Every analysis grounds in the raw IR deck (SharePoint URI on `analyses.source_uri`) or is explicitly labelled `synthesis-from-cycles` / `reconstructed-from-...` / `derived-from-...`. Version bump = methodology change; content update = dated log entry, no bump. Every DB change → `version_log` + `change_log`. Deck-printed values only in `items`; derivations flagged in `meta`.
